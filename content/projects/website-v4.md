The fourth iteration of <a href="https://howardt12345.com/" target="_blank">howardt12345.com</a>, rebuilt from the ground up with Vue 3 and Nuxt 4, hosted on Vercel. Features a dual-purpose portfolio and photography showcase: the dev side includes a Timeline of work experience, Featured Projects, and a filterable projects gallery; the photography side serves a full masonry gallery with category and tag filtering powered by a Supabase backend. Also adds dark/light theme switching, bilingual support (English and Traditional Mandarin) via i18next, and scroll-based section tracking.
